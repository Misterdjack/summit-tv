Services

The Smart Speaker System

Sonos is a system of HiFi wireless speakers and audio components. It unites your digital music collection in one app that you control from any device. Play what you want in every room over a dedicated wireless network.

Nest reinvents unloved but important home products, like the thermostat and the smoke alarm. We focus on simple, beautiful and thoughtful hardware and services.

WiFi Thermostats gives you remote control of your homes HVAC system. Turn the heat up on a cold day on the mountain and come home to your cozy mountain home. Most people leave the house at one temperature and forget to change it. So Nest Learning Thermostat learns your schedule, programs itself and can be controlled from your phone. Teach it well and the Nest Thermostat can lower your heating and cooling bills up to 20%.
